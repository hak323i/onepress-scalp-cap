# OnePress Scalp Cap

**The world's first open-source, one-press, full-scalp microneedling device.**

A soft, flexible medical-grade silicone cap you simply place on your head and press once with both hands for complete, uniform scalp coverage in under 60 seconds. No rolling. No missed spots. No hair snagging.

![OnePress Scalp Cap Concept](concept.jpg)

## ⚠️ CRITICAL SAFETY WARNING — READ BEFORE DOWNLOADING OR USING

This is an **experimental research prototype only**.  
Microneedling creates micro-channels in the skin and carries **real medical risks** including:

- Serious infection (especially on the scalp with hair follicles)
- Scarring, keloids, or permanent hair loss
- Bleeding, prolonged inflammation, or allergic reactions
- Transmission of blood-borne pathogens if not properly sterilised

**You must:**
- Use **only medical-grade materials** (platinum-cure silicone + surgical stainless steel or titanium needles)
- Start with **0.25–0.5 mm needle length maximum**
- Sterilise everything thoroughly (autoclave preferred, or medical-grade disinfectant + UV)
- Never use on broken skin, active infections, irritated scalp, or if you have bleeding disorders
- Consult a dermatologist or doctor before any use
- This device is **not approved** by any regulatory body (FDA, CE, MHRA, etc.)

**For personal/experimental use only. Not for sale, clinical use, or distribution as a finished medical device.**

## Why This Design Is Better Than Everything Else

- **One single press** = full uniform scalp coverage
- **30–60 seconds** total treatment time (vs 10–20 minutes with rollers)
- **Zero hair damage** — no rolling or dragging
- **Perfectly even pressure** every time
- **Better serum absorption** — apply minoxidil, peptides, exosomes, or growth factors immediately after

No commercial product currently exists that does this.

## Technical Specifications (v1.0)

**Base Material**  
Platinum-cure medical-grade silicone, Shore A 20–40 hardness (Smooth-On Ecoflex 00-30 or equivalent)

**Needles**  
- Material: Surgical stainless steel or titanium  
- Length: 0.5 mm (start here — do not go longer until experienced)  
- Gauge: 30–33G  
- Density: 0.8–1.2 needles per mm² (\~900–1,200 total needles)  
- Pattern: Even square or hexagonal grid across entire inner surface (leave 15 mm clean rim)

**Dimensions**  
- Rim circumference: 56–62 cm (highly stretchy — one size fits most adults)  
- Crown-to-rim height: 18–22 cm  
- Base thickness: 3–5 mm  
- Needle protrusion: exactly 0.5 mm inward

**Optional Future Features**  
- Integrated micro-channels between needles for serum flow  
- 650 nm red-light LED array  
- Replaceable needle modules  
- Different sizes (S/M/L)

## How to Build Your First Prototype

### 1. Digital Model (Free)
**Recommended:** FreeCAD (free & powerful)  
**Quick alternative:** Tinkercad (browser-based)

**Tinkercad quick steps:**
1. Go to tinkercad.com → New Design
2. Create a sphere (radius \~95 mm) and cut off the bottom 40%
3. Add a short cylinder for the sides
4. Hollow the shape (wall thickness 4 mm)
5. Round all edges
6. Export as STL

**Needle placement:** Do **not** model 1,000+ individual needles in the 3D file. The digital model is the **cap base only**. Needles are added during physical prototyping.

### 2. Physical Prototype Options

**Option A – Fast rigid test version**  
3D print the STL. Test fit and pressure distribution.

**Option B – Flexible version (recommended)**  
- Buy a cheap silicone swim cap  
- Laminate commercial microneedle patches or disassembled dermaroller heads onto the inside  
- Bond with medical-grade silicone adhesive

**Option C – Community build**  
Open an Issue on this repo asking for help with silicone casting or makerspace access.

## Usage Protocol (Experimental)

1. Clean and disinfect scalp + hair thoroughly
2. (Optional) Apply numbing cream 20–30 minutes prior
3. Place cap on head and adjust for snug fit
4. Press firmly and evenly with both palms for 30–60 seconds
5. Remove cap
6. Immediately apply topical treatment (minoxidil, peptides, etc.)
7. Clean and sterilise cap thoroughly after every use
